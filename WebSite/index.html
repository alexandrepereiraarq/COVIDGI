COVID GI

Covid-19 als “Perfekter Sturm” in Brasilien

COVID-19 has created a "Perfect Storm" in Brazil. The first year of the pandemic provided one of the most significant examples of the impact on health, politics and society. During the last couple of months, the health care system has crashed, the political system has threatened to collapse, and pre-existing poverty has multiplied many times over. How can data help circumvent this "perfect storm"?

The research team of COVIDGI is working in three Brazilian cities, collecting a wide range of data to understand vulnerabilities to COVID-19 in health, politics, and society. These data include anonymized behavioural data (e.g. from social networks or research) and official data. After this data research, the team will develop a toolkit that can support decision making in similar situations, plus they will model how secure management of current and future pandemics can be aimed and designed.

How can decision-making on #coronavirus benefit from more agile and precise information through authoritative, open, and VGI-sourced spatial behavioral data? Our team at CLISEC will research the COVID-19 pandemic in three Brazilian cities.
